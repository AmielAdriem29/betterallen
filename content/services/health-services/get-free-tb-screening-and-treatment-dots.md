# Get Free TB Screening and Treatment (TB-DOTS) - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU provides TB consultation, diagnosis support, treatment enrollment, and monitoring under TB-DOTS.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information    | Details                                                                                  |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Office         | Rural Health Unit                                                                        |
| Who can apply  | Residents of Allen                                                                       |
| Service type   | Government to Citizen                                                                    |
| Coverage       | Screening, physician assessment, GeneXpert referral, treatment enrollment, and follow-up |
| Estimated fees | **TB treatment is free**; diagnostics like chest X-ray may have external fees            |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Visit RHU for TB symptoms or follow-up

- Report prolonged cough, blood in sputum, weight loss, or related symptoms.
- Staff will register your details and assess your condition.

### Step 2: Complete physician and diagnostic process

- You will undergo vital signs and physician consultation.
- You may be referred for GeneXpert, chest X-ray, or other tests.

### Step 3: Enroll in TB-DOTS if diagnosed

- TB coordinator registers eligible patients in the National Tuberculosis Program.
- You will receive medicine guidance and treatment monitoring.

### Step 4: Attend regular follow-ups

- Return as scheduled for treatment checks and adherence monitoring.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certificate)
- [ ] Previous medical records, lab results, and prescriptions (if available)
- [ ] Chest X-ray PA result when required

### Eligibility

- Residents of Allen only.
- Priority lane support is available for persons with disability, pregnant women, and senior citizens.
