# Enroll in PhilHealth YAKAP Outpatient Services - Municipality of Allen

The Allen RHU is a primary facility accredited under PhilHealth YAKAP and supports eligible outpatient enrollment and consultation.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information               | Details                                                             |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit                                                   |
| Who can apply             | Residents of Allen eligible for YAKAP enlistment                    |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen                                               |
| Coverage                  | Enrollment support, outpatient consultation, and medicine provision |
| Estimated processing time | Around 23 minutes per client                                        |
| Fees                      | None                                                                |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register at RHU admission section

- Provide your personal details and present supporting documents.

### Step 2: Complete eligibility and enlistment process

- RHU staff records your information and checks eligibility for YAKAP enrollment.
- Vital signs are taken before consultation.

### Step 3: Proceed to consultation and medicine release

- Physician consultation is provided.
- Medicines are given based on clinical findings and availability.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certification)
- [ ] Birth certificate and marriage certificate (if applicable)
- [ ] PhilHealth Member Data Record (MDR)
- [ ] Certificate of indigency when required

### Eligibility

- Residents of Allen only.
