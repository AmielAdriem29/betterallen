# Get General Medical Consultation and Basic Emergency Care - Municipality of Allen

The Rural Health Unit (RHU) in Allen provides outpatient consultations for children and adults, including minor emergency care.

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Go to the RHU admission area

- Share your personal details and health concern.
- If you are a first-time patient, staff will create your patient record.

### Step 2: Proceed to vital signs assessment

- RHU staff will check your weight, temperature, and blood pressure.
- Pregnant clients may also be assessed for gestational details.

### Step 3: See the physician

- The doctor will review your history and perform a physical examination.
- You may be referred for lab tests or diagnostics if needed.

### Step 4: Claim medicines and instructions

- Proceed to the RHU dispensary.
- Staff will explain dosage, frequency, and duration if medicines are available.

### Step 5: Record and follow-up

- RHU staff updates your consultation record.
- Return on your scheduled follow-up date, if advised.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certification)
- [ ] Previous medical records, laboratory results, and prescriptions (if available)

### Priority and Emergency Lane

- Persons with disability, pregnant women, and senior citizens are assisted through a priority lane.
- Minor emergency cases (such as cuts, burns, breathing difficulty, or loss of consciousness) receive immediate intervention.
