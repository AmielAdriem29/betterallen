# Access Prenatal and Child Health Services - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU and barangay health stations provide prenatal care and child health support from pregnancy through early childhood.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information               | Details                                                                     |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit and Barangay Health Stations                              |
| Who can apply             | Women of reproductive age and children up to 5 years old                    |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen                                                       |
| Coverage                  | Prenatal consult, birth plan support, vitamins advice, follow-up scheduling |
| Estimated processing time | Around 26 minutes per client                                                |
| Fees                      | None                                                                        |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Visit RHU or barangay health station

- Register your details and reason for consult.

### Step 2: Undergo prenatal or child assessment

- Staff checks health history, vital signs, and pregnancy or child growth indicators.
- Pregnant clients receive monitoring for expected date of confinement and age of gestation.

### Step 3: Receive care plan and schedule

- RHU staff assists with birth planning, vitamins, self-care guidance, and follow-up dates.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certification)
- [ ] Previous medical records, lab results, and prescriptions (if available)

### Eligibility

- Women of reproductive age and children age 5 and below.
