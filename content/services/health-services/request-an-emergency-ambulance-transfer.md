# Request an Emergency Ambulance Transfer - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU provides emergency ambulance support for eligible referrals and urgent transfers.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information               | Details                                                |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit                                      |
| Who can apply             | Residents of Allen                                     |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen                                  |
| Estimated processing time | Around 20 to 60 minutes per client; travel time varies |
| Fees                      | None                                                   |

---

## How to Request

### Step 1: Coordinate referral details

- The referring clinic or hospital should call the receiving hospital first.
- Provide diagnosis, patient status, and reason for referral.

### Step 2: Submit required records

- Prepare valid ID and medical records.
- For hospital-to-hospital transfer, include referral confirmation to receiving hospital.

### Step 3: Wait for ambulance dispatch and transfer

- RHU prioritizes emergencies within its jurisdiction.
- For unstable patients, the referring institution should provide a nurse escort when needed.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certification)
- [ ] Referral to receiving hospital for hospital-to-hospital transfer
- [ ] Medical records, lab results, and prescriptions for home-to-hospital transfer

### Important Conditions

- RHU prioritizes local emergency cases first.
- Financial and discharge obligations at the referring facility should be settled before pick-up.
