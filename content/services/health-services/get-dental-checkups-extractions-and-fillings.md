# Get Dental Checkups, Extractions, and Fillings - Municipality of Allen

The Allen RHU Dental Clinic provides **assessment**, **tooth extraction**, **filling**, and **gum** care services.

---

| Information               | Details                                                               |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit Dental Clinic                                       |
| Who can apply             | All                                                                   |
| Schedule                  | Every Wednesday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM (subject to dentist availability) |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen                                                 |
| Estimated processing time | Around 25 minutes per client (excluding queue time)                   |
| Possible fees             | Tooth extraction may cost Php 100.00; other services may be free      |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register at the dental clinic

- Share your personal information.
- Have your initial assessment and vital signs checked.

### Step 2: Receive the proper dental procedure

- Services may include extraction, permanent or temporary filling, and gum cleaning.
- The dentist will explain treatment and aftercare instructions.

### Step 3: Get prescription and follow-up advice

- If needed, the dentist issues a prescription.
- Return for scheduled follow-up checks.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certification)
- [ ] Previous dental or medical records, lab results, and prescriptions (if available)

### Eligibility

- All residents and clients are welcome.
- Priority lane support is available for persons with disability, pregnant women, and senior citizens.
