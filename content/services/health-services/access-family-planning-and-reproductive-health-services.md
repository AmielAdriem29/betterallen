# Access Family Planning and Reproductive Health Services - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU provides counseling and family planning methods for couples and individuals of reproductive age.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information               | Details                                                               |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit                                                     |
| Who can apply             | Couples and individuals of reproductive age                           |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen                                                 |
| Coverage                  | Family planning counseling, method selection, and commodity provision |
| Estimated processing time | Around 22 minutes per client                                          |
| Fees                      | None                                                                  |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register at RHU

- Present your ID and share your basic information.

### Step 2: Attend family planning counseling

- RHU staff explains available methods, benefits, and possible effects.
- You can ask questions and choose a method suitable for your needs.

### Step 3: Receive selected method and follow-up schedule

- RHU provides the selected method or commodity based on availability.
- Return for follow-up or resupply as advised.

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- Couples and individuals of reproductive age in Allen.
