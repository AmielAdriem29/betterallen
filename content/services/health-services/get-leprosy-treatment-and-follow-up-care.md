# Get Leprosy Treatment and Follow-up Care - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU provides consultation, treatment regimen support, counseling, and follow-up monitoring for leprosy cases.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information               | Details                      |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit            |
| Who can apply             | Residents of Allen           |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen        |
| Estimated processing time | Around 42 minutes per client |
| Fees                      | None                         |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register at RHU admission area

- Provide your details and relevant case history.

### Step 2: Complete vital signs and physician consult

- RHU staff performs initial assessment.
- Physician reviews records and confirms treatment plan.

### Step 3: Receive counseling and treatment regimen

- RHU explains medication schedule, dose, and duration.
- Follow-up visits are scheduled for continued monitoring.

### Step 4: Continue follow-up and record updates

- RHU records your case progress and updates your treatment follow-up.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certification)
- [ ] Previous medical records, lab results, and prescriptions (if available)

- Residents of Allen only.
