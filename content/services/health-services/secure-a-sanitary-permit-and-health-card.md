# Secure a Sanitary Permit and Health Card - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU and the sanitation office issue sanitary permits, health cards, and related certificates for businesses and food handlers.

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Request assessment and required tests

- Visit the Rural Sanitary Inspector office.
- Food handlers may be asked to complete laboratory tests.

### Step 2: Undergo inspection

- Sanitation staff inspect your workplace and hygiene practices.
- Business inspection may include water supply, storage, waste disposal, and comfort room conditions.

### Step 3: Complete compliance and claim documents

- Submit all required test results and inspection requirements.
- Claim your sanitary permit, health card, or inspection report once approved.

---

## Requirements

### Documents and Diagnostics

- [ ] Laboratory tests (CBC, urinalysis, fecalysis, sputum exam, Hepatitis B screening) as required
- [ ] Chest X-ray PA result when required

### Eligibility

- Food handlers and owners of sari-sari stores, bakeries, eateries, piggeries, poultry, and similar establishments in Allen.
- Priority lane support is available for persons with disability, pregnant women, and senior citizens.
