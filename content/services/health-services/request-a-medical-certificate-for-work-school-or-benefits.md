# Request a Medical Certificate for Work, School, or Benefits - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU issues medical certificates for residents who need health clearance or supporting documents.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information               | Details                                                                                                                              |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit                                                                                                                    |
| Who can apply             | Residents of Allen                                                                                                                   |
| Common use cases          | Fit to work, fit to participate in school activities, excuse for absence, medico-legal, PWD ID support, financial assistance support |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen                                                                                                                |
| Estimated processing time | Around 15 minutes per client (excluding queue time and external diagnostics)                                                         |
| Fees                      | None                                                                                                                                 |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register at RHU

- Provide your personal information and reason for requesting a certificate.

### Step 2: Undergo assessment

- If you are a new consultation case, the municipal health officer may perform an assessment.
- For fit-to-work or fit-to-participate requests, chest X-ray may be required.

### Step 3: Claim your certificate

- Once requirements are complete and reviewed, proceed to releasing.
- RHU staff issues your medical certificate.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Valid ID (government ID, school ID, employment ID, or barangay certification)
- [ ] Previous medical records, lab results, and prescriptions (if available)
- [ ] Chest X-ray PA result (for fit-to-work or fit-to-participate requests)

### Eligibility

- Residents of Allen only.
- Priority lane support is available for persons with disability, pregnant women, and senior citizens.
