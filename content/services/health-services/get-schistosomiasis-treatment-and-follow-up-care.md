# Get Schistosomiasis Treatment and Follow-up Care - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU provides schistosomiasis consultation, counseling, medicine access, and follow-up support for residents.

| ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register and initial interview

- RHU staff records your personal information and case history.

### Step 2: Vital signs and physician consultation

- You will undergo basic assessment and medical review.
- The physician confirms findings and gives treatment instructions.

### Step 3: Receive counseling and medicine

- RHU provides counseling and follow-up guidance.
- Praziquantel and related instructions are provided through the dispensary.

### Step 4: Return for follow-up monitoring

- Follow the schedule set by RHU for reassessment and continuing care.
