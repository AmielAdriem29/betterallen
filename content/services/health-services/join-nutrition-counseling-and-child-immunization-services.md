# Join Nutrition Counseling and Child Immunization Services - Municipality of Allen

Allen RHU supports child nutrition recovery, nutrition counseling, and routine immunization for vaccine-preventable diseases.

---

## What You Need to Know

| Information               | Details                                                                                |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Office                    | Rural Health Unit                                                                      |
| Who can apply             | Children 0 to 59 months, children 5 years and older, and caregivers                    |
| Service type              | Government to Citizen                                                                  |
| Coverage                  | Nutrition assessment, counseling, referral, and Expanded Program on Immunization (EPI) |
| Estimated processing time | Around 25 minutes per client                                                           |
| Fees                      | None                                                                                   |

---

## How to Apply

### Step 1: Register at RHU

- Bring child information and available health records.

### Step 2: Get nutrition or immunization assessment

- Staff checks weight, height, and child health status.
- Children due for vaccines receive age-appropriate immunization.

### Step 3: Receive counseling and follow-up schedule

- Caregivers are guided on nutrition, home care, and next visits.
- RHU sets future monitoring or vaccination appointments.

---

## Requirements

### Documents Needed

- [ ] Birth certificate
- [ ] Medical records and vaccination records (if available)

### Eligibility

- Children in Allen, including those needing nutrition intervention or routine immunization.
